# Blockchain Basics – Web3 Learning Path (India)

A beginner-friendly blockchain learning roadmap covering fundamentals, architecture, and real-world use cases, with a focus on India.

## Who is this for?

This learning path is designed for:
- Students new to blockchain and Web3
- Engineering / CS students
- Developers transitioning into Web3
- tech-curious learners exploring blockchain use cases in India

No prior blockchain experience required.

## What you will learn

By the end of this path, you will understand:
- How blockchain works under the hood
- Core concepts like decentralization, consensus, and cryptography
- Differences between public, private, and consortium blockchains
- How blockchain is applied in finance, governance, and India’s digital ecosystem

## Curriculum Overview

### 1. Blockchain Fundamentals & Architecture
- What is a blockchain?
- Blocks, chains, and transactions
- Decentralization and P2P networks
- Consensus mechanisms (PoW, PoS)
- Cryptography basics (hashing, signatures)
- Blockchain vs traditional databases
- Smart contracts (intro)
- Benefits and limitations of blockchain

### 2. How Blockchain Works (Technical Deep Dive)
- Transaction lifecycle and validation
- Mining and block creation
- Block rewards and incentives
- Forks and consensus upgrades
- Gas fees and transaction costs
- Nodes and network synchronization
- Using block explorers
- Example transaction walkthrough

### 3. Blockchain Applications & Use Cases in India
- Government initiatives (DigiLocker, land records)
- Banking, fintech, and payments
- Supply chain and agriculture
- Healthcare and pharma tracking
- CBDC (Digital Rupee)
- Indian startups and ecosystem
- Policy challenges and future outlook
